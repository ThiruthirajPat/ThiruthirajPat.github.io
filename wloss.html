    • The Workshop Leader

    Lavanya Sunassy-Pather

    Lavanya Sunassy-Pather has more than 12 years’ experience in Dietetics and Nutrition. Holder of a BSc in Nutrition, Food Service Management and Dietetics and an MSc in Clinical Nutrition, she notably served as Head of the Dietetics Department at the Apollo Bramwell Hospital (now Wellkin Hospital), the largest private hospital in Mauritius.

    In the first half of 2019, she intervened as Dietitian in the 10th Edition of Indian Ocean Island Games, conducting counselling, meal planning and follow-ups for circa 300 Mauritian athletes over a period of 3 months.

    Since 2012, she has been accompanying both individuals and organisations with a view to promoting balanced and healthy food habits and enabling sustainable nutritional changes among the Mauritian population.

    The core domains of intervention of Lavanya include:

  • Diet management for diabetes
  • Diet management for children with autism
  • Diet management for the elderly persons
  • Paediatric nutrition
  • Enteral & parenteral nutrition
  • Renal nutrition
  • Prenatal, intrapartum and postpartum nutrition
  • Nutrition education & development of individual dietary plans
  • Weight management
