He is the 1st person in Mauritius to be awarded the Certified International Wealth Manager designation, a graduate-level professional qualification offered by the International Association of Wealth Management (Switzerland).

He holds a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration & Management, a Master in Management/Diplôme Grande Ecole and an MSc in European Business from the EDHEC Business School, a triple-crowned and leading "Grande Ecole de Commerce" in France, an L.LM. in International Business Law from the University of Paris II Panthéon-Assas (France), and a Certificate in Full-stack Web Development (BAC+3) from Le Wagon. Thiruthiraj also completed executive courses in strategy and negotiation at INSEAD and HBX I Harvard Business School.

    Since 2005, Thiruthiraj contributed over 35 articles on economic and business topics to the following newspapers/magazines: Mauritius Times, Le Défi Economie, Discover & Invest, Capital, L’Express and Le Mauricien.
