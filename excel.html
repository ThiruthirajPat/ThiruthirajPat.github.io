The Workshop Leader

Thiruthiraj Pather
Experience

A former academic scholar of the French Government, Thiruthiraj Pather is the Founder of Lapidus Azuri Consulting Ltd, a Business & Management Consulting firm. He combines a strong strategic and analytical mind with a unique set of competencies and experiences in the fields of corporate strategy, management, corporate finance, M&A, private equity, business intelligence, international business law, private sector development and full-stack web development. Over the past 15 years, he has collaborated with large foreign and Mauritian enterprises, public-sector organisations, SMEs and NGOs. He has also been lecturing, at graduate and postgraduate levels, on entrepreneurship, finance and strategic supply chain management. In 2016-2017, Thiruthiraj played a pivotal role as Key Expert in the formulation of the '2017-2026 National Master Plan for the SME Sector in Mauritius'. In 2012-2015, he was involved, as Corporate Manager – Investments (Group), in the re-organisation of the Rogers Group and the setting-up of Rogers Capital. In 2010-2012, as Market & Economic Studies Manager (Group), he effectively led the restructuring of the business intelligence function and the deployment of the group revenue management system at Lux Resorts. Thiruthiraj was a member of the Executive Council of the Mauritian Institute of Management and the Chairman of the Committee on Industrialisation of the Policy Forum. He is the 1st person in Mauritius to be awarded the Certified International Wealth Manager designation, a graduate-level professional qualification offered by the International Association of Wealth Management (Switzerland). In 2001-03, he completed a 2-year “Classe préparatoire aux Grandes Ecoles de Commerce” (specialisation in economics). He holds a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration & Management, a Master in Management/Diplôme Grande Ecole and an MSc in European Business from the EDHEC Business School, a triple-crowned and leading "Grande Ecole de Commerce" in France, an L.LM. in International Business Law from the University of Paris II Panthéon-Assas (France), and a Certificate in Full-stack Web Development (BAC+3) from Le Wagon. Thiruthiraj also completed executive courses in strategy and negotiation at INSEAD and HBX I Harvard Business School. Since 2005, contributed over 35 articles on economic and business topics to the following newspapers/magazines: Mauritius Times, Le Défi Economie, Discover & Invest, Capital, L’Express and Le Mauricien.
